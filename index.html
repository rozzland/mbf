What we do?

It is not every day that an opportunity presents itself to make a difference in the Moving & Relocation Industry. Movers’ Best Friend – 3rd Party Services is our oppurtunity to provide you with the Personal Touch & Commitment to make your job easier. MBF offers you a full range of services covering the 50 states & Canada

have been involved in the 3rd party service industry for over 16 years in various management positions in sales, customer service, quality assurance and even some hands on field work. I have had the oppurtunity to crisscross the USA visiting move coordinators, reloaction specialists, and field technicians and attending national & state conventions. Also, as a member of the American Movers and Storage Association (AMSA) & the Employee Relocation Council (ERC) I have earned recognition as a Cenrtified Moving Consultant (CMC) and Certified Relocation Professional (CRP) respectively.
